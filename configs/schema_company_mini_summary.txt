Schema C (SPB company mini-slice)

Classes:
- dbo:Company (company entity)
- gn:Feature (location entity)
- foaf:Person (key person entity)

Relations (use only these):
- dbo:location (Company -> Location)
- dbo:industry (Company -> Industry IRI)
- dbo:keyPerson (Company -> Person)
- dbo:foundingYear (Company -> literal year)
- dbo:numberOfEmployees (Company -> literal count)
- rdfs:label, spb:prefLabel (labels for entities)
- foaf:name (Person -> name literal)
- rdf:type (type triples)

Notes:
- Industry resources may not have explicit rdf:type; treat industry as an entity IRI with labels.
- Use only the allowed predicates and types listed below.
